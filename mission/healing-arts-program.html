Healing Arts Programs
About the Program
Our programs aim to revive faith in nature of child victims of natural disasters.
We believe that children who survive disasters must be allowed to be the central actors in rebuilding their communities.

Our approach:
• We motivate the affected community to focus on its future needs and not just on restoring the past that is lost.
• We focus on family as the most important social unit and the school as the community center for post-recovery.
• We deepen children’s emotional bonds with the place where they grew up to offset the painful memories of the disaster.
• We aid and accelerate children’s emotional recovery so that their resilience and optimism can revitalize the community.
